App Description: The idea of this app is to provide a simple platform for users to buy, sell, and rent clothing locally from other users around them. Users interested in selling or renting items can simply list their item under the "add listing" page. People interested in buying or renting an item can view their feed and click items for more details (including how to get in contact with the seller of the item). Items listed on Local Apparel will be filtered by location to ensure that only items within a specific proximity to a user will be shown.
